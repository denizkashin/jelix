# Jelix

`Jelix` is a neon one-finger swipe survival game where reflexes decide everything.

You stay inside **The Lane**, avoid hazards, and chase high scores while speed keeps rising.

## Why players like Jelix

- **One-finger controls**  
  Fast and intuitive swipe gameplay designed for quick sessions.

- **High-intensity survival loop**  
  Every run is a balance of risk and control as the game accelerates.

- **Distinct characters and skins**  
  Unlock and play with different styles that change the feel of each run.

- **Story flavor and codex progression**  
  Discover themed content and build your in-game codex over time.

- **No ads, no subscription**  
  Core experience stays clean. Optional content is available as one-time purchases.

## How to play

1. Open `Jelix`.
2. Swipe to keep your character in the safe lane.
3. Dodge hazards and keep your streak alive.
4. Improve your timing and beat your best score.

## Monetization

- No recurring subscription model.
- No third-party ads in core gameplay.
- Optional one-time in-app purchases for unlockable content.
